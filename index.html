Where the money goes

Revenue looks great — until RTO, COD and returns hit.

These are the costs that quietly turn your best-sellers into break-even — and they hit Indian D2C stores hardest. Margin OS bakes every one into your true profit, automatically.

RTO (return-to-origin)

Failed COD deliveries cost you shipping both ways plus handling — with zero revenue to show for it.

COD fees & failures

Cash-on-delivery is most of your orders and the least of your margin once charges and non-delivery pile up.

Returns & refunds

Every return is the refund plus lost shipping and restocking — and it's almost never counted per product.

Payment & gateway fees

2–3%+ shaved off every prepaid order before you ever see a rupee of it.

Shipping you absorb

"Free shipping" is a very real per-order cost. Margin OS counts it on every order.

Product cost (COGS)

The big one — what each SKU actually costs you to source or make, subtracted per order.

Margin OS subtracts your product cost, returns, shipping and fees on every single order — so the profit lost to RTO and failed COD finally shows up in your real numbers.
