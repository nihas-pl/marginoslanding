Questions

Everything you're wondering.

What does Margin OS actually calculate?
Your true net profit — revenue minus COGS, transaction fees, shipping, refunds, and your operating expenses — for every order, product, and channel. Not just revenue.
How much does it cost?
$9.99/month with a 7-day free trial, billed through Shopify on your regular invoice — no separate card needed. Waitlist members get founding-merchant pricing.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and used only to calculate your profit. We access only what's needed — your orders, products, and the costs you enter. If you uninstall, your data is deleted within 48 hours.
Do I have to set anything up?
Margin OS syncs your orders and products automatically on install. You just add your product costs — inline or by bulk CSV — plus any monthly expenses, and your real margins appear instantly.
Which stores does it work with?
Any Shopify store. It installs in one click from the Shopify App Store and runs embedded right inside your admin — nothing else to connect.
When does it launch?
It's in final review with Shopify right now. Join the waitlist and you'll be first to get access the moment it goes live.
